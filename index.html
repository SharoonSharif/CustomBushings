Born From Frustration

In June 2014, the shock bushings on Derek Goodlin's 1999 Polaris Sportsman 500 wore out. After searching everywhere, he discovered the frustrating truth: nobody sold replacement bushings. Polaris wanted him to buy entirely new shocks.

"I refused to pay hundreds of dollars for new shocks when all I needed was a $25 bushing. So I made my own."

With his engineering background, Derek machined the first Custom Shock Bushings in his Arkansas shop. Word spread through the Polaris community, and what started as a personal fix became the go-to solution for thousands of ATV and UTV owners.

Today, with his sons helping run the family business, Custom Shock Bushings continues to operate from the same shop where it all beganâ€”still Made in USA, still backed by the owner who rides the same machines you do.
